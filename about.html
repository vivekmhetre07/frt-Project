ABOUT US

Helping people make better seafood choices for a healthy ocean
We protect the ocean now and for the future, through trusted seafood recommendations and collaboration with businesses, governments, consumers and partners worldwide.

WHAT WE DO ?

Our work is global in focus and impact. Our standards and recommendations are central pillars of the global sustainable seafood movement that companies rely on when purchasing seafood. We also work directly with producers who want to improve their practices and report on the state of sustainable seafood for government bodies, including the United Nations and the United States Congress.
